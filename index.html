>>> FEW FACTS ABOUT EARTH

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet that supports life. About 71% of its surface is covered by water, while the remaining 29% consists of land, including continents and islands. It has a breathable atmosphere rich in nitrogen and oxygen, which protects living organisms and helps regulate temperature. Earth rotates on its axis once every 24 hours, causing day and night, and revolves around the Sun in about 365.25 days, creating the seasons. It is home to millions of species of plants, animals, and microorganisms, making it a unique and diverse planet. Protecting Earth's environment through reducing pollution, conserving natural resources, and preserving biodiversity is essential for ensuring a healthy future for all living beings.

About the Earth

Top three fact about Earth

  • Earth is the only planet known to have liquid water on its surface.
  • Earth has a strong magnetic field that protects it from harmful solar radiation.
  • Earth's atmosphere is composed mainly of nitrogen (78%) and oxygen (21%), with trace amounts of other gases.
